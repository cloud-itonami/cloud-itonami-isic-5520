# Governance

cloud-itonami-isic-5520 follows open-source governance with transparent
decision-making documented in ADRs.

Scope: Back-office site-occupancy logging, facility maintenance scheduling,
supply-restock coordination, guest-safety-concern escalation.

Out-of-scope: Directly issuing/executing evacuation orders, directly
contacting emergency medical services or law enforcement, and overriding
guest-safety-authority decisions.
